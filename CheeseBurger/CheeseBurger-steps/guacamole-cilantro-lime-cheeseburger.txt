Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat.
To make the guacamole, mash the avocado in a medium bowl with the juice of half a lime, jalapeno, tomatoes, 1/4 cup onion, and 1 1/2 teaspoons of minced garlic; season to taste with salt, and set aside.
In a large bowl, mix together beef, the juice of half a lime, 1 tablespoon garlic, chili powder, 1/2 cup diced onion, and cilantro.
Form the meat into 6 patties.
Cook the burgers to desired doneness on the preheated grill.
Add a slice of cheese to each burger during the last minute of cooking.
Serve on toasted buns with a dollop of guacamole.
