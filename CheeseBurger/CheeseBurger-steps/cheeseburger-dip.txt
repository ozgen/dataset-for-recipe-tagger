Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
Cook and stir sweet onion and garlic in the hot oil until the onion is tender, about 5 minutes.
Break ground beef into the skillet; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes more.
Reduce heat to medium-low.
Stir chili sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper into the beef mixture.
Pour cheese spread into the skillet; cook and stir until the cheese spread is melted evenly through the beef mixture, 3 to 5 minutes.
Transfer beef mixture to a serving dish.
Layer lettuce, tomatoes, and pickles atop the beef mixture to serve.
