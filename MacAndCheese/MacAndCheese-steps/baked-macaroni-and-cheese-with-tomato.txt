Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Pour in pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
In large bowl, combine macaroni, soup, milk, cheese and 6 tablespoons butter.
Pour into 9x13 baking dish.
Top with bread crumbs and dot with remaining butter.
Bake for 45 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly.
