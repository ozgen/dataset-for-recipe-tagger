Cook and drain pasta as directed on package; keep warm.
Meanwhile, in 2-quart saucepan, heat cooking sauce to boiling, stirring occasionally.
Remove from heat; stir in cheese until melted.
Reserve 1 tablespoon of the bacon; set aside.
Stir in pasta and remaining bacon.
If necessary, cook over medium heat until thoroughly heated, stirring occasionally.
Mix reserved bacon with bread crumbs; sprinkle over pasta.
